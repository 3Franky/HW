How to shave a beard!

Shaving a beard is a very common thing to do, but some do not know how to shave!

Steps in order to shave.

  • shaving cream
  • after shave cream

Method

  1. wet face with lukewarm water
  2. apply shaving cream
  3. shave with the grain
  4. apply after shave
